About Us

TechNot Coin (🔗TNT) is a forward-thinking cryptocurrency designed for the tech-savvy and innovation enthusiasts. Focused on integrating blockchain technology with cutting-edge tech sectors like AI, IoT, and Quantum Computing, TechNot aims to bridge the gap between digital currency and the latest technological advancements.

Token Distribution

  • Public Sale: 50% 🛒: Available for purchase by the general public to encourage widespread adoption and liquidity.
  • Development and Research: 20% 🛠️: Allocated for ongoing research, development, and innovation in technology integration.
  • Founders and Team: 10% 👥: Reserved for the founding team, vested over time to align with long-term project goals.
  • Marketing and Promotions: 10% 📣: Earmarked for marketing campaigns, community building, and promotional activities.
  • Strategic Partnerships: 5% 🤝: For forging strategic alliances with tech companies and industry leaders.
  • Reserve Fund: 5% 🏦: Set aside as a reserve for unforeseen expenses or future projects.
